functionalities

Personal settings

User Registration

  • Plans > Medical
  • Select the plan of your preference.
  • Complete the registration form.
  • Enter the code that automatically arrives in the email with which you registered.

Make the payment for your plan

  • Once registration is complete, log in to your SQLAPIO account
  • Add a payment method.
  • Select the plan of your preference.

Complete your registration

  • Complete the required user information.
  • Load the digital signature.
  • Saves the entered data.

Load seal and digital signature

  • Upload the image with your seal and signature.
  • They must be in PNG or JPEG format.
  • The image must have the doctor's signature and the seal with the tuition numbers.

Enter the system with a registered user from the web

  • Menu > Enter the system > Medical System
  • Sign in to your SQLAPIO account.

Associate a medical center

  • Click on the Associate Center button.
  • Select your center.
  • Complete the center information.
  • Saves the entered data.

Associating a new medical center

  • Click on the Associate Center button.
  • Select the New Center option.
  • Complete the center information.
  • Saves the entered data.

Registering a new patient

  • Complete the required patient information.
  • Load the patient image.
  • Saves the entered data.

CLINICAL HISTORY

  • Complete the required patient information.
  • Saves the entered data.

PHYSICAL EXAM

  • Complete the required patient information.
  • Saves the entered data.

Medical Consultation

  • Complete the required patient information.
  • Saves the entered data.

Diagnosis with AI

  • Select or enter the patient's symptoms.
  • Click on the Consultation with Artificial Intelligence button.
  • You can copy the results to paste them into the diagnosis.

Scheduling an appointment for a patient

  • Complete the required appointment information.
  • Schedule the appointment.

Scheduling an appointment for an unregistered patient

  • Complete the required patient and appointment information.
  • Schedule the appointment.

Loading or Viewing Exams

  • Search for the patient to whom the exam will be added.
  • Click on the Load Exams button.
  • Select the results to upload, it can be 1 or several.
  • Upload the file in JPEG or PDF.
  • Saves the changes made.

Loading or Viewing Studies

  • Search for the patient to whom the study will be added.
  • Click on the Load Studies button.
  • Select the results to upload, it can be 1 or several.
  • Upload the file in JPEG or PDF.
  • Saves the changes made.
